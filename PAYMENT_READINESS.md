# Payment Readiness

## Goal

Prepare the business for paid audits without handling payments inside the AI system.

## Payment options

Use a trusted external payment link or invoice tool.

Recommended safe flow:

1. Client confirms scope.
2. Send proposal.
3. Send payment link or invoice manually.
4. Start audit after payment confirmation.
5. Deliver audit report within agreed timeline.

## Do not automate

- card handling
- wallet actions
- trading
- refunds
- payment disputes

## Audit start checklist

- client name
- project description
- repo or system overview
- current problem
- desired outcome
- delivery date
- payment status

## Delivery promise

Starter audit: 48 hours after payment confirmation.
