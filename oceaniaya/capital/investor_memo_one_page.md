# Oceaniaya — One-Person AI-Native Digital Company

## One-line thesis

Oceaniaya is building private, audit-ready AI operating systems for wealthy, regulated and premium European sectors, starting with Switzerland and the Netherlands.

## Why now

Companies want AI efficiency, but regulated and premium businesses cannot simply paste sensitive work into generic tools. They need private workflows, human approval boundaries, documented risk controls, and measurable service or operations outcomes.
