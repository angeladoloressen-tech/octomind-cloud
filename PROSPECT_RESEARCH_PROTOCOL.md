# Prospect Research Protocol

## Purpose

Turn raw ecosystems into manually reviewed named prospects.

## Inputs

- Account Radar 20
- Automation Agency Prospecting 20
- Named Prospect Queue
- public websites
- public case studies
- public partner directories

## Verification checklist

A prospect can move from research to qualified only if:

- public website exists
- services match automation, CRM, operations, no-code or AI workflows
- business clients are likely
- outreach can be personalized from public information
- no private data is used
- no bulk sending is planned

## Score

Hot:

- clear automation services
- AI or workflow complexity
- agency or consulting business model
- likely budget
- visible case studies

Warm:

- automation-adjacent
- some operations language
- unclear AI angle

Cold:

- generic web agency
- no automation signal
- no business buyer signal

## Output

Each verified prospect gets:

- account name
- website
- evidence note
- pain hypothesis
- personalization hook
- recommended offer
- next action

## Rule

No outreach without manual review.
