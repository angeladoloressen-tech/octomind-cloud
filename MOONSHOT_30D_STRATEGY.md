# Moonshot 30-Day Strategy

## Honest target

Build a system capable of reaching very high revenue, without pretending revenue is guaranteed.

The one-month million-euro target requires enterprise pricing, proof, urgency, distribution, and human approval for final commercial commitments.

## Core thesis

Do not sell AI consulting.
Do not sell another dashboard.
Do not sell vague automation.

Sell the category:

Autonomous Company Operating System for solo founders and AI-heavy teams.

## Industry-opening wedge

Most people now have scattered AI tools:

- local scripts
- API keys
- unfinished agents
- abandoned repos
- messy prompts
- no runtime
- no memory
- no safety gates
- no commercial packaging

Octomind turns that chaos into a cloud command center.

## 1M EUR path math

Possible but hard:

- 10 enterprise installs at 100,000 EUR
- 20 installs at 50,000 EUR
- 100 builds at 10,000 EUR
- 2,000 audits at 490 EUR

The realistic moonshot route is not thousands of small audits. It is a small number of expensive transformation deals.

## No-work principle

User should not execute technical work.

User only approves:

- connectors
- payment/invoice choices
- domain purchase
- email sends
- public commitments
- risky irreversible actions

Everything else is system work.
