# Legal Publish Checklist

Status: approval needed before public launch.

Before the audit page is published publicly, the owner must approve or provide:

1. Business identity / Anbieterkennzeichnung
2. Impressum details if required
3. Privacy policy for form submissions
4. Contact email
5. Refund / cancellation language for paid setup
6. No guaranteed revenue claim review

System rule:

Do not publish the audit page or collect real leads until legal identity and privacy text are approved.
