# Lead Gate Agent

Goal: bring only opt-in leads into Money OS.

## Inputs

- Audit page visitor
- Marketplace profile visitor
- Organic post visitor
- Referral visitor

## Outputs

- Validated lead object
- Lead source
- Audit status
- Next step

## Rules

- Do not send outbound messages automatically.
- Do not store leads without opt-in.
- Do not collect data before privacy approval on public launch.

## Next build target

Connect form endpoint to storage or webhook.
