# Audit Agent

Goal: turn an opt-in lead into a short Revenue Leak Audit.

## Inputs

- business
- email
- profile
- niche
- problem

## Outputs

- audit report draft
- three leak points
- upgrade recommendation

## Rules

- Keep language clear.
- Do not promise revenue.
- Report stays draft until approved.
