# Industry Expansion Protocol

Status: active.

## Purpose

Teach Money OS how to enter new industries without creating product chaos.

## Core rule

New industries are allowed only as vertical lenses on the same core funnel:

Revenue Leak Audit -> AI Revenue Autopilot Setup -> Monthly Support.

## What changes per industry

- Pain language
- Audit checklist
- Example leaks
- Trust signals
- Delivery templates
- Traffic channel

## What does not change

- Payment spine
- Approval gates
- No revenue guarantee
- No unapproved outreach
- No random product creation

## Expansion loop

1. Select industry.
2. Score urgency and ability to pay.
3. Build industry audit checklist.
4. Build traffic asset draft.
5. Build delivery template.
6. Test one channel after approval.
7. Keep or stop based on real signals.

## Current emergency constraint

Do not launch new industries publicly until the first Money OS lead capture path is approved and deployed.
