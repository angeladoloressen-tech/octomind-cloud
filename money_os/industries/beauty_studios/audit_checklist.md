## Three common leaks

### Leak 1: Attention without booking path

The profile may look good, but the next booking step is not obvious enough.

### Leak 2: Manual DM chaos

Requests arrive in DMs, comments, WhatsApp, or calls, but they are not structured into a clean booking pipeline.

### Leak 3: Weak repeat appointment loop

Happy clients are not consistently asked to rebook, review, or refer.
