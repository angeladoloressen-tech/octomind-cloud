## Greece

### Best angles

1. Tourism-linked beauty and wellness studios
2. Small hotels, villas, tour operators, and local experience providers
3. Clinics and appointment-based health/wellness providers
4. Restaurants and beach clubs with weak reservation/follow-up systems
5. Property and short-stay operators needing inquiry sorting

### Why Greece

Greece has strong inbound travel, hospitality demand, and many small service businesses that depend on fast inquiry response and reviews.

### First contact categories

- Boutique hotel groups
- Spa and beauty studios in Athens, Thessaloniki, Mykonos, Santorini, Crete
- Local tour and experience providers
- Digital agencies serving tourism clients
- Appointment platforms and health/wellness directories

### Example firms/platforms to study or approach carefully

- Doctoranytime, as a Greek appointment/health platform reference
- Greek Travel Pages / tourism directories as discovery source
- Local digital agencies serving hotel and beauty clients

## Turkey

### Best angles

1. Beauty clinics, hair transplant clinics, dental clinics
2. Beauty salons and aesthetic centers in Istanbul, Antalya, Izmir, Ankara
3. Medical tourism agencies
4. Hotels and tourism operators
5. E-commerce and local service SMEs

### Why Turkey

Turkey has large tourism volume, strong medical/beauty tourism, and a very active Instagram/WhatsApp-driven local business culture.

### First contact categories

- Hair transplant clinics
- Dental and aesthetic clinics
- Beauty salons and chains
- Tourism agencies
- Local marketing/CRM agencies

### Example firms/platforms to study or approach carefully

- USHAS / HealthTürkiye ecosystem as market reference
- Booksy and similar appointment platforms as competitive reference
- Local clinic directories and Istanbul/Antalya medical tourism agencies

## Dubai / UAE

### Best angles

1. Premium beauty and wellness studios
2. Aesthetic clinics and dental clinics
3. Hospitality, events, guest-intake workflows
4. Real estate brokers and property management teams
5. Home services and concierge businesses
6. Luxury retail and mall-based service providers

### Why Dubai

Dubai has premium service density, high tourism volume, expat demand, luxury positioning, and many appointment/inquiry-heavy businesses.

### First contact categories

- Premium beauty salons and spa groups
- Aesthetic clinics
- Hospitality and event operators
- Real estate agencies
- Home services marketplaces
- Digital agencies serving luxury/local brands

### Example firms/platforms to study or approach carefully

- ServiceMarket as UAE home-services marketplace reference
- Letswork as platform partnership reference
- Dubai Chamber / local business directories as discovery sources
- Salon and wellness booking platforms as integration or partnership references

## Language strategy

- Greece: English first, then Greek later if traction appears.
- Turkey: Turkish plus English for medical tourism.
- Dubai: English first, Arabic optional later, Hindi/Urdu optional for operator-side workflows later.

Do not contact companies automatically. Use this map to build approved landing pages, traffic assets, and opt-in partner routes.
