# Multilingual Market Protocol

Status: active.

## Core rule

Money OS must be English-first, then localized by target country.

English is the master commercial language. Local-language versions are market adaptations, not separate products.

## Default language stack

1. English master copy
2. German for Germany, Austria, Switzerland
3. Turkish for Turkey and Turkish-speaking local businesses in Germany
4. French for France, Belgium, Switzerland
5. Spanish for Spain and Latin America tests
6. Dutch for Netherlands and Belgium
7. Italian for Italy

## What gets localized

- landing page copy
- traffic post copy
- audit checklist examples
- Canva social assets
- marketplace listing drafts
- email draft templates
- delivery report summary

## What stays unchanged

- core funnel
- pricing logic unless country test requires separate pricing
- no-guarantee language
- approval gates
- payment verification rule

## Core funnel

Revenue Leak Audit -> AI Revenue Autopilot Setup -> Monthly Support.

## Localization rule

Translate meaning, not word-for-word. Use local business language and local channel habits.

## Safety rule

No public posting or customer messages without approval per country and channel.
