# Regulated Finance Gate

Purpose: allow Money OS to explore direct money businesses without crossing into illegal banking, investment, lending, or payment activity.

## Core rule

Money OS may build finance software, payment workflows, merchant analytics, revenue automation, invoice tooling, and marketplace infrastructure.

Money OS must not operate as a bank, broker, lender, e-money institution, payment institution, or investment adviser unless proper licensing, legal review, and approved regulated partners exist.

## Blocked without legal approval

- Taking deposits
- Holding customer funds
- Issuing bank accounts
- Offering loans or credit
- Moving third-party money outside approved payment processors
- Investment recommendations
- Trading customer assets
- Crypto custody or exchange
- E-money issuance

## Allowed first-stage finance products

1. Merchant revenue audit
2. Cashflow cockpit
3. Payment link and invoice automation
4. Subscription recovery and churn prevention
5. Marketplace referral system using licensed payment processors
6. Stripe Connect-style platform planning
7. Finance ops reports for small businesses

## Safe north star

Build a finance-adjacent operating system that improves business cashflow without acting as the regulated financial institution.
