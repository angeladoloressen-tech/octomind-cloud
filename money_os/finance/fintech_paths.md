# Fintech Paths for Money OS

Purpose: move beyond small local service sales while staying inside safe payment infrastructure.

## Decision

Money OS should become a finance-adjacent platform before attempting anything regulated.

## Path A: Cashflow Cockpit

For small companies that need simple revenue visibility.

Value:
- revenue state
- payment link status
- invoice discipline
- follow-up alerts
- monthly review

Revenue:
- setup fee
- monthly software fee
- reporting add-ons

## Path B: Payment Link OS

For small companies that need simple productized payment flows.

Value:
- offer page
- payment link
- intake
- delivery workflow

Revenue:
- setup fee
- monthly fee

## Path C: Marketplace Layer

For service providers that sell through a shared system.

Value:
- provider profile
- checkout
- intake
- delivery tracking

Revenue:
- platform fee
- subscription fee

Needs:
- licensed payment processor
- legal review before launch

## Path D: Finance Ops Copilot

For owners who need daily business money discipline.

Value:
- daily money report
- unpaid invoice tracking
- payment link suggestions
- follow-up alerts

Revenue:
- monthly fee
- premium setup

## First move

Build Cashflow Cockpit and Payment Link OS first.

Use approved payment processors as the payment layer.
