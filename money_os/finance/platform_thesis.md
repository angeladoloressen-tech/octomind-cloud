# Money OS Finance Platform Thesis

Purpose: move beyond street-market-style service sales into a repeatable finance operations platform.

## Category

Money OS is not a bank. Money OS is a finance operations layer for small businesses.

## Customer pain

Small businesses do not need more dashboards. They need money discipline:

- what is paid
- what is unpaid
- what offer converts
- which follow-up is missing
- which payment link should be sent
- which client should be retained

## Platform wedge

Start with Revenue Leak Audit, then expand into:

1. Cashflow Cockpit
2. Payment Link OS
3. Finance Ops Copilot
4. Marketplace Layer with approved payment processor infrastructure

## First scalable product

Cashflow Cockpit for local businesses.

## Why this can scale

The same workflow repeats across niches:

lead -> audit -> offer -> payment -> intake -> delivery -> review -> support.

## Safety

Do not hold customer funds. Do not provide credit. Do not give investment advice. Use approved payment processors for payments.
