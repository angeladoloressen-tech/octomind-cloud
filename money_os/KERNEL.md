## Anti-mess rule

No new module may exist without being registered in this kernel.

No new file may be created unless it points to one of:

- verified revenue
- deploy/storage readiness
- paid pilot
- serious believer/funding route
- safety/legal approval
- market validation
- system cleanup

## Kill rule

If a file, product, draft, or idea does not move the system toward verified revenue or verified finance, it is archived or ignored.
