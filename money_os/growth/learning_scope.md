# Money OS Learning Scope

Purpose: define what Octomind is allowed to learn and optimize.

## What the system learns

Money OS learns commerce, not speculative trading.

It learns:

1. Lead generation
2. Offer clarity
3. Trust signals
4. Pricing power
5. Payment conversion
6. Delivery speed
7. Client retention
8. Monthly recurring revenue
9. Cash state visibility
10. Funnel bottlenecks

## What it does not do by default

Money OS does not act as a stock, crypto, forex, or investment trading bot.

It must not:

- gamble with money
- execute trades
- promise returns
- make investment decisions for the owner
- move money without approval

## Finance meaning inside Money OS

Finance means business finance:

- confirmed revenue
- pending payments
- monthly recurring revenue
- refund risk
- churn risk
- acquisition cost
- profit margin
- cash runway

## Commerce meaning inside Money OS

Commerce means:

- find a customer entry point
- diagnose a business problem
- present one clear offer
- collect payment
- deliver the service
- retain the client

## Current learning target

The system should learn how to turn the Revenue Leak Audit into a reliable inbound customer acquisition funnel.

## Truth boundary

Confirmed revenue is only revenue visible in Stripe, bank, or accounting records.
