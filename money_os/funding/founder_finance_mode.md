# Founder Finance Mode

Status: active.

## Purpose

Money OS needs a human believer, not only customers.

The system should prepare for five finance paths:

1. Angel believer
2. Strategic sponsor
3. Accelerator
4. Public grant
5. Paid pilot partner

## Core story

Octomind is a one-person digital company operating system. It builds and coordinates revenue, launch, payment, workflow, and learning loops through connected tools.

## Current truth

Stripe balance is 0. The system has assets and payment links, but needs launch approval, storage, deploy target, traffic, and a first believer.

## Funding ask draft

Target first finance: 25k to 100k EUR or USD.

Use of funds:
- legal and company setup
- deploy and storage infrastructure
- first paid traffic and market testing
- founder runway
- product packaging
- first pilot delivery

## Rule

No false billionaire claims. Present ambition as a moonshot and evidence as evidence.
