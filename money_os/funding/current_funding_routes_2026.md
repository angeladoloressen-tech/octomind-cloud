# Current Funding Routes 2026

Status: research draft, not submitted.

## Purpose

Keep Octomind focused on realistic finance paths instead of vague hope.

## Route A: NRW Gruendungsstipendium

Fit: early founder support in North Rhine-Westphalia.

Observed facts:
- Monthly support is listed as 1,200 EUR for one year.
- The programme includes coaching.
- Jury sessions are paused from 01.04.2026 for application processing, but submitted applications continue to be processed and jury sessions are expected to resume later.

Octomind action:
Prepare eligibility check and idea paper, but do not assume acceptance.

## Route B: EIC Accelerator

Fit: later-stage EU route if Octomind becomes a strong innovation and scale case.

Observed facts:
- EIC Accelerator is for startups and SMEs with innovative, game-changing products, services, or business models that can create or disrupt markets.
- Grant component can be below 2.5M EUR.
- Investment component can be 1M to 10M EUR.
- Selection involves short proposal, full proposal, and face-to-face interview.

Octomind action:
Not first route. Build evidence first.

## Route C: Hub71 / Abu Dhabi ecosystem

Fit: Dubai/UAE and Abu Dhabi expansion, AI startup ecosystem, partner access.

Observed facts:
- Hub71 lists 410+ startups and 200+ partners.
- Access Programme includes in-kind and cash incentives and access to capital/corporate partners/mentors.
- Hub71+ AI is designed for AI startups and offers technical resources, AI network access, and strategic partners.

Octomind action:
Prepare Hub71-style application narrative after deploy page and demo flow are presentable.
