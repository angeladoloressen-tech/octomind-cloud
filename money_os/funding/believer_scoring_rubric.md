# Believer Scoring Rubric

Status: active draft.

## Purpose

Score potential believers before any outreach.

## Score factors

Each factor is scored 0 to 5.

1. Pain understanding
2. Ability to fund or open doors
3. Relevance to AI operations or service businesses
4. Reachability
5. Trust fit
6. Strategic value
7. Country or market alignment

## Score bands

- 28 to 35: priority target
- 20 to 27: good target
- 12 to 19: maybe later
- 0 to 11: ignore for now

## Disqualifiers

- asks for money upfront to introduce investors
- requires unrealistic promises
- pushes unlicensed finance activity
- wants guaranteed revenue claims
- asks for secrets, seed phrases, or unsafe access

## Rule

Do not send outreach without approval. Score first, personalize second, send only after approval.
