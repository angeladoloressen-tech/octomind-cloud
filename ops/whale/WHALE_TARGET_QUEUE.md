# Whale Target Queue

Date: 2026-05-25
System: Whale Revenue Desk
Default public language: English

## Purpose

Create a practical first queue of whale-style targets and target types.

The goal is not to spam. The goal is to find companies where a small public proof sample can show a real signal-path issue.

## Primary whale target types

### Tier 1: AI sales and revenue workflow companies
Why:
They already understand sales teams, AI agents, response speed, pipeline quality, and revenue operations.

Proof sample angle:
Inspect public demo or contact path. Identify one friction point in how a buyer moves from curiosity to qualified conversation.

Offer path:
Free proof sample -> Executive Signal Audit -> Revenue Command Sprint.

### Tier 2: AI productivity hardware brands
Why:
They often need affiliate, reseller, partner, creator, and product discovery workflows.

Proof sample angle:
Inspect affiliate/partner route, product page clarity, creator application route, or regional program selection.

Offer path:
Free proof sample -> Partner route audit -> OctoAmazonas affiliate/product route.

### Tier 3: premium venues and event groups
Why:
A single event inquiry can be high-value. Slow or unclear intake can leak revenue.

Proof sample angle:
Inspect event inquiry route, booking form, corporate events page, or follow-up clarity.

Offer path:
Free proof sample -> Executive Signal Audit -> Event inquiry command map.

### Tier 4: boutique agencies and creative-tech studios
Why:
Agencies lose money when leads, briefs, proposals, or follow-ups are scattered.

Proof sample angle:
Inspect contact/project inquiry flow and proposal readiness.

Offer path:
Free proof sample -> Signal Audit -> Revenue Command Sprint.

### Tier 5: B2B SaaS with demo request flows
Why:
Demo paths, onboarding, trial conversion, and partner inquiries are measurable high-value workflows.

Proof sample angle:
Inspect demo request page and first next-step clarity.

Offer path:
Free proof sample -> Executive Signal Audit -> Enterprise pilot.

## Qualification before outreach

Do not contact a target until all are true:

1. Public page reviewed.
2. One specific friction point found.
3. One small improvement written.
4. No private data invented.
5. No guaranteed revenue claim.
6. Contact route is legitimate.
7. Message is under 180 words.
