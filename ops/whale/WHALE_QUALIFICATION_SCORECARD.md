# Whale Qualification Scorecard

Date: 2026-05-25
System: Whale Revenue Desk

## Purpose

Stop chasing weak prospects. Score targets before spending effort.

## Scoring

Start with 0.

Add points:

+5 company has clear high-value inquiry flow
Examples: demo request, booking, enterprise sales, partner contact, event inquiry, reseller page.

+5 company has team or department complexity
Examples: sales team, partnerships team, support team, events team, agency team, operations team.

+4 public pain is visible
Examples: confusing contact route, weak follow-up language, many forms, unclear partner path, scattered calls to action.

+4 product or service value is high
Examples: B2B SaaS, luxury venues, AI hardware, agencies, enterprise workflows, event bookings.

+3 likely budget exists
Examples: paid product, premium positioning, enterprise pricing, established brand.

+3 diagnostic can be done from public information first
No credential requirement for first proof sample.

+2 target fits Octomind or OctoAmazonas route.

Subtract points:

-5 no business process visible
-5 no public contact route
-4 likely no budget
-4 requires regulated advice or sensitive credentials too early
-3 only wants free custom work
-3 too tiny or hobby-only
-3 unclear buyer
-2 no way to send legitimate first contact

## Priority bands

P0 Whale: 14+ points
Action: create proof sample and outreach.

P1 Whale Watch: 9 to 13 points
Action: keep in target queue, prepare light proof if capacity exists.

P2 Weak: 4 to 8 points
Action: do not spend active effort unless a reply appears.

Park: below 4 points
Action: ignore until new signal appears.

## Required target record fields

- Company
- Segment
- Website
- Public signal path
- Pain hypothesis
- Score
- Proof sample angle
- Suggested offer
- Contact route
- Risk note
- Next action

## Conversion rule

Do not sell the 490 EUR diagnostic first if the company has never seen proof.

Sequence:
1. Free proof sample
2. Executive Signal Audit
3. Revenue Command Sprint
4. Enterprise Pilot

## Safety rule

No guaranteed revenue claims.
No invasive analysis.
No credential request before paid scope.
No pretending to know private metrics.
