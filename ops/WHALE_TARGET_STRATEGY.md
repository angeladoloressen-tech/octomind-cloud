# Whale Target Strategy

Date: 2026-05-25
System: Octomind / Whale Revenue Desk

## Decision

The target is not small fish.
Small low-ticket offers may warm up trust, but the main business direction is larger clients with real budgets and operational pain.

## Target customer

Whales are organizations with:
- teams
- sales or partner workflows
- slow follow-up
- messy lead intake
- multiple tools
- missed revenue signals
- budget for diagnostics or pilots
- enough complexity to value a command-center map

## Not the target

Avoid relying on:
- random tiny buyers
- one-off hobby creators
- people who need heavy hand-holding but cannot pay
- vague dreamers with no business process
- leads that only want free work

## Offer ladder for whales

### 1. Proof Sample
Price: free
Purpose: give a small useful proof without exposing internal system secrets.
Output: one missed signal, one leak, one next action.

### 2. Executive Signal Audit
Price: 490 EUR
Purpose: paid diagnostic for serious teams.
Output: workflow leak map, priority fixes, risk notes, next-action report.

### 3. Revenue Command Sprint
Price: 1,490 EUR
Purpose: pilot-ready setup map for a specific revenue workflow.
Output: offer route, lead capture, follow-up path, payment path, automation map.

### 4. Enterprise Revenue OS Pilot
Price: custom
Purpose: larger engagement after diagnostic or sprint.
Output: scoped pilot plan, implementation brief, integration map, executive summary.

## Sales psychology

Use customer-foot-in-the-door flow:
1. Show small proof.
2. Build credibility.
3. Offer paid diagnostic.
4. Move only serious buyers to pilot.

## Public language

Default homepage language must be English.
German and Turkish can be available as optional language choices.

The public page should not feel like a cheap micro-service shop.
It should feel like a serious operational diagnostic desk.

## Outreach targets

Prioritize:
- boutique agencies
- event venues
- creator-tech companies
- affiliate teams
- AI hardware brands
- local premium service businesses
- B2B SaaS teams
- companies with public partner/affiliate/contact pages

## Core promise

No guaranteed revenue.
No vague implementation promise.
No spam.

Promise:
- find leaks
- map the signal path
- identify the next action
- reduce operational fog
- prepare a pilot path
