## Fastest options

### 1. Bank transfer / invoice

Best if the founder can issue or send simple payment details.

Needs:

- legal name or business name
- IBAN or payment details
- invoice or payment note
- tax/VAT clarity if applicable

### 2. PayPal

Best if already available.

Needs:

- PayPal payment link or PayPal email
- product text
- delivery terms

### 3. Wise

Best for international bank-style payments.

Needs:

- Wise payment link or payment details
- currency
- delivery terms

### 4. Stripe

Best for professional payment link.

Needs:

- Stripe account
- product: Cloud Command Center Diagnostic
- price: 490 EUR
- success page or confirmation text

### 5. Gumroad / Lemon Squeezy

Best for simple productized diagnostic.

Needs:

- product name
- price
- description
- delivery promise
