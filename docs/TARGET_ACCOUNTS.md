## Outreach rule

Do not run bulk cold email.

Use these accounts to build:

- search-intent pages
- public proof pages
- partner angles
- launch posts
- directory positioning
- controlled one-to-one business inquiries only when relevant and lawful

## Account scoring

Score each account by:

- inquiry volume potential
- lost-follow-up pain
- ability to pay
- speed to decision
- fit with a 49 EUR audit
