## The wedge

Millions of people see products in videos but cannot answer:

- Is it real?
- Where do I buy it?
- Is the link safe?
- Is there a better alternative?
- Is the product repeated, fake, risky, or unavailable?
- Which brand owns the real product?

OctoAmazonas answers those questions and monetizes both sides:

1. Buyers pay for product verification.
2. Brands pay for verified discovery and launch placement.
3. Creators and publishers use OctoAmazonas as a product link intelligence layer.
4. Agencies and ecommerce operators buy trend reports and lead lists.
