# OctoAmazonas — Demo First Strategy

## Decision

Yes. Use a demo before asking for money.

The first outreach should not be a cold sales email asking for 199 EUR. The safer and stronger route is:

1. Create a public or private demo listing.
2. Send it through official affiliate, partner, reseller, media, or contact forms.
3. Ask for the right route or permission to list.
4. Convert interest into affiliate listing, featured placement, or launch pack.

## Why demo first works better

A demo reduces friction because the brand can immediately see the value.

Instead of saying:

"Pay us 199 EUR."

Say:

"We prepared a preview listing. Is this the right affiliate or partnership route for approval?"

## First demo target

Start with PLAUD Note Pro because it is easy to explain:

- AI productivity product
- strong visual hardware
- fits Future Gadgets and AI Gadgets
- likely to have buyer curiosity from videos
- good candidate for affiliate listing

## Demo page structure

### Header

PLAUD Note Pro — AI note-taking device

### Labels

- Category: AI Productivity
- Status: Demo Preview
- Verification: Needs brand confirmation
- Monetization path: Affiliate Listing / Featured Product / Launch Pack

### Buyer promise

Find the gadget from the video, verify the buy link, compare alternatives, and understand whether it is useful.

### Sections

1. What it is
2. Why buyers search for it
3. Where to buy
4. Alternatives
5. Risk and verification note
6. Brand partnership CTA

## Demo CTA for brands

"Is this listing accurate, and should we use your official affiliate or partner link?"

## Safe contact-form message

Hello,

I am building OctoAmazonas, a curated discovery portal that helps people find and compare useful gadgets they see in online videos.

I prepared a demo preview listing for {{product_name}} because it looks like a strong fit for our Future Gadgets collection.

Could you point me to the right affiliate, partner, reseller or media application route for approval?

Best,
OctoAmazonas

## Conversion ladder

1. Brand replies with official affiliate program.
2. Add affiliate link to product card.
3. Publish verified listing.
4. Track clicks.
5. Offer Featured Product at 49 EUR/week.
6. Offer Octo Launch Pack at 199 EUR after proof of listing quality.

## Rule

Do not sell before showing the demo. The demo is the bridge from unknown project to credible partner.
