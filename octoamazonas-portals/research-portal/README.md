# OctoAmazonas Research Portal

Internal research portal for turning viral gadget content into structured product intelligence.

## Goal

Capture product candidates from YouTube descriptions, comments, TikTok clips, blogs, newsletters, Reddit posts, Amazon lists, and creator videos.

## Research workflow

1. Add source title and URL.
2. Extract product names and links.
3. Classify each product.
4. Add comment signals.
5. Mark missing links.
6. Score trend potential.
7. Send promising products to verification.
8. Send verified products to buyer portal.
9. Send brand candidates to vendor outreach.

## Source fields

- Source title
- Platform
- Channel / creator
- Source URL
- View count
- Date
- Product count
- Has affiliate links
- Missing link complaints
- Safety complaints
- Price complaints
- Buyer intent comments

## Product candidate fields

- Product name
- Category
- Raw source line
- Buy URL
- Source timestamp
- Comment signal
- Trend score
- Verification status
- Revenue path

## Comment signal types

- Link missing
- Price requested
- Product fake concern
- Safety concern
- Strong buyer intent
- Alternative requested
- Medical or assistive need
- Repeated product complaint

## Priority rule

Highest priority products are not the flashiest ones. They are the ones with buyer intent, missing link demand, clear utility, and safe monetization.
