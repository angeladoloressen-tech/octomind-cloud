## Goal

Find manually reviewed named agencies, consultants, and partner ecosystems that already sell automation or operations work.

## Offer angle

AI Operations Reset for agencies and consultants whose delivery is becoming scattered across tools, workflows, clients, APIs, approvals, and documentation.

## Next step

For each ecosystem, identify one real named candidate and verify:

- website exists
- services match automation or ops
- likely has business clients
- outreach can be personalized
- no private data is used

## Rule

No bulk sending. No scraping private data. No outreach without manual review.
