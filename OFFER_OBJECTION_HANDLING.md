# Offer Objection Handling

## Objection: This is too expensive

Answer:

The smaller diagnostic is 490 EUR and is designed to reduce risk before a larger reset. The high-ticket reset is only for systems where scattered operations are already costing time, delivery quality, or client trust.

## Objection: We already use automation tools

Answer:

That is exactly why the reset exists. The problem is not tool access. The problem is coordination, governance, documentation, approvals, memory, and delivery rhythm across tools.

## Objection: We can build this internally

Answer:

Possible. The audit can still shorten the path by mapping what to keep, what to remove, what should move to cloud, and where risk sits.

## Objection: We are not ready

Answer:

Then the 48-hour diagnostic is the safer first step. It creates the map without forcing a large implementation decision.

## Objection: What is the ROI?

Answer:

The first ROI is operational clarity: less breakage, less key-person dependency, clearer approval paths, faster delivery, and a stronger service offer.

## Rule

Do not overpromise. Do not guarantee revenue. Sell clarity, speed, safety, and implementation leverage.
