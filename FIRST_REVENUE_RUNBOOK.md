# First Revenue Runbook

## Mission

Get the first paid diagnostic without bulk sending or risky automation.

## Offer

Cloud Command Center Diagnostic

Price: 490 EUR
Timeline: 48 hours

## Daily loop

1. Pick one ecosystem from Automation Agency Prospecting 20.
2. Find one named candidate from public sources.
3. Verify services match automation, CRM, operations or AI workflows.
4. Write one personalization hook.
5. Prepare one reviewed outreach draft.
6. Send only after human approval.
7. Track reply in dealboard.
8. If interested, offer diagnostic.
9. If price objection, use 490 EUR diagnostic as risk-reduction step.
10. If qualified, send proposal.

## Target

First paid diagnostic.

## Success metrics

- 20 researched candidates
- 10 personalized drafts
- 5 approved sends
- 2 replies
- 1 paid diagnostic

## Rule

Do not chase everyone. Find the sharpest pain and make one precise offer.
